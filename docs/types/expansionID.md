# Type: expansionID

← [API Types](../API Types.md) · [WoW API Docs](../index.md)

---

Numeric indication of what expansion pack is currently installed on the given client. Higher numbered expansions include all lower expansions.

- `0` - World of Warcraft (no expansions installed)
- `1` - World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade
- `2` - World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King
- `3` - World of Warcraft: Cataclysm
